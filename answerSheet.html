Question 1


A person by the name of EpicMan124 likes to play video games a lot. He really likes 1 game in particular, and deicded that he wanted to try and level up in the game as much as he can. Due to this, he made a scedule for how many points he should get in an hour. He started off with 5 points, and wants to gain 2 points every hour. He ends up playing for 7 hours, and each hour he got the desiered amout of points. How amny points does EpicMan124 have now? Write an algebraic expression to find out.

Answer = EpicMan124 now has 19 points in total. An algebraic expression would be 5+p. "p" represents the points that are earned in total. Solving the expression is pretty simple. To find "p," we need to find out how many points EpecMan124 made in the total 7 hours that he played. We can do that by simply doing 2*7=14. That means that p=14. 5+14=19. Therefore EpicMan124 now has 19 points in total.

Emmily has 2 red disks and 1 blue disk. Every day she gets 3 more blue disks. How many disks will Emily have after 7 days? Write an algebraic expression.

Answer = Emily will have 2 red disks and 22 blue disks after 7 days. An algebraic expression would to find the amount of blue disks would be 1+d. "d" represents how many disks Emily earns over the 7 days. To solve "d" we need to do some basic math. 3*7=21, therefore d=21. 1+21=22, therefore Emily would have 22 blue disks after the 7 days. Since no red disks are earned every day, the red disks stay at 2.
